  2. A Fielding Restriction Area (FRA)shall be marked on the field of play. It shall consist of two semi-circles with their centres asthe middle stumps at either end of the pitch, the radius of each of the semi-circlesshall be 30 yards(27.43 metres) and linked by two parallel straight lines. No part of the FRA shall encroach within 10 yards of a boundary and, if space is restricted, it should be marked at that point irrespective of the radius above.
